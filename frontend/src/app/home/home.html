About Our Barangay

Barangay Zone 43, located in the heart of Sampaloc, Manila, is a vibrant and thriving community dedicated to serving its residents with integrity, compassion, and excellence. Our barangay has been a cornerstone of the local community for decades, fostering unity, progress, and sustainable development.

We are committed to providing essential services, organizing community programs, and maintaining a safe and harmonious environment for all residents. Our dedicated officials work tirelessly to address the needs of our community members, ensuring that everyone has access to quality public services and opportunities for growth.

Through various initiatives, programs, and events, we strive to build a stronger, more connected community where every resident feels valued and supported. Together, we continue to work towards a brighter future for Barangay Zone 43.
